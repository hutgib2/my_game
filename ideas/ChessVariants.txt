idea 1: chess but all pieces are queens except for the king.
idea 2: chess but all pieces are pawns except for the king.
idea 3: chess reboot

chess reboot pieces:
replacements: 
1. peasant replaces pawn
2. dragon replaces knight
3. archer replaces queen
4. wizard replaces bishop
5. catapult replaces rook
6. emperor replace king

peasant:
can only move one even on its first move and there's 8 of them.
can only move and kill forwards. 
can promote if it reaches other sides of map.

dragon:
can move up to 2 spaces in all directions per turn and there's 2 of them.
can move through enemy piece if there's an empty tile.

archer:
only 1 archer on board and can move 1 tile in all directions per turn but cannot kill by moving.
can kill an enemy up to to 3 tiles away every turn but no one can move on the same turn.

wizard:
can move 1 tile in all directions per turn but cannot move an opponent on same turn.
can move any enemy and ally piece to any tile every 2 turns but no one can move on the same turn.
cannot move stunned enemies and allies and there are 2 of them.

catapult:
can move 1 tile in all directions per turn but cannot stun on same turn.
can stun any enemy to any tile for 2 turns every 3 turns but no one can move on the same turn.
cannot stun enemies that were just moved and there are 2 of them.

emperor:
only one emperor and can move like the queen piece but 
player will lose if the emperor is put in checkmate.

chess board is still the same.
